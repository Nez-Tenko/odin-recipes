Omelette Recipe

A Recipe for a Delicious Omelette

This will be a quick tutorial on how to make an omelette! Please make sure to follow all of the steps as is! Thank you and hope you enjoy!

Ingredients
  • 3 large fresh eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon cold water
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 pinch cayenne or white pepper
Steps
  1. Whisk eggs, water, and salt together in a bowl.
  2. Heat 1 1/2 tablespoons butter in skillet over medium high heat.
  3. Pour in whisked eggs
  4. Flip egg after the bottom has cooked
  5. Remove omelette once egg has fully cooked
