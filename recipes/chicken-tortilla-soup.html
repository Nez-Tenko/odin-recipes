Chicke Tortilla Soup

A Recipe For Delicious Chicken Tortilla Soup

This will be a quick tutorial on how to make chicken tortilla soup! Please make sure to follow all of the steps as is! Thank you and hope you enjoy!

Ingredients
  • Whole Chicken
  • 3 White Onions
  • 1 Bunch Cilantro
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 1/2 Whole Black Peppercorns
  • 1 Teaspoon Mexican Oregano
  • 2 Dried Chili Pepper Pods, Seeded
  • 3 Quarts Water
  • 12 Corn Tortillas
Steps
  1. Combine chicken, onion, stems from cilantro, etc, in a large soup pot.
  2. Cut tortillas in half and bake in oven.
  3. Let soup simmer.
  4. Add cut tortillas to soup.
