Grilled Cheese

A recipe for a delicious grilled cheese sandwich

This will be a quick tutorial on how to make a grilled cheese sandwich! Please make sure to follow all of the steps as is! Thank you and hope you enjoy!

Ingredients
  • White bread
  • Cheddar
  • Butter
Steps
  1. Butter the bread on one side. Place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top
  3. Butter top piece of bread
  4. Flip sandwich
  5. Cook until the cheese is melted
